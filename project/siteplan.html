Website Planning Document - Andrey Blanco

Site Name:

JW fitness

jwfitnessprograms.cr

JW is the acronym for Johan Wallace (my nephew). He is a renowned fitness trainer with over 20 years of experience in the area. He is the owner of "Outdoorfit - Training Camp," a place where many people gather for guidance and physical training. With the restrictive measures imposed by Covid-19, Johan Wallace has been migrating his services to the virtual world. He now needs a website to promote his training programs so that users can follow them from their homes or favorite locations. I want to use this project as a first glimpse into what a JW Fitness website could be. Given the years of experience and the recognition Johan has cultivated in recent years, we believe JW Fitness is sufficient for him to be identified on the website.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this page is to bring all the training programs and plans developed by Coach Johan Wallace to a virtual platform. The page will feature JW's plans, programs, and exercises. Users will be able to create their own exercise routine guided by Johan Wallace.
